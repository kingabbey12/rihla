# Privacy Policy — Rihla

_Last updated: 2026-06-27. Effective on first public release._

This Privacy Policy explains how Rihla ("the App", "we") handles your information. Rihla is privacy-first: by default the App does not send analytics or crash data anywhere.

## 1. Information we process

### On your device (not transmitted by default)
- **Location** — used to show your position, calculate routes, and provide navigation and safety advisories. Used only while you use the App ("in-use").
- **Medical profile** — optional information you enter (e.g. blood type, allergies, conditions, medications) to assist emergency responders.
- **Emergency contacts** — names and numbers you choose to add.
- **Preferences** — theme, language, units, and feature settings.

### Cloud sync (only if you create an account)
- If you sign in, your saved places, preferences, and selected data are synchronized to our cloud provider (Supabase) so you can use them across devices. Authentication tokens are stored encrypted on your device.

### Optional analytics & crash reporting (off by default)
- If a build is configured with analytics/crash reporting enabled, the App may send **sanitized** product events (e.g. "journey started") and crash diagnostics. Before anything leaves the device, we remove personal identifiers, tokens, email addresses, phone numbers, and precise coordinates.

## 2. How we use information
- To provide navigation, search, offline maps, exploration, safety advisories, and emergency features.
- To synchronize your account data (if you sign in).
- To diagnose crashes and improve reliability and usability (only with analytics/crash reporting enabled).

We do **not** sell your personal information. We do **not** use your data for advertising.

## 3. Emergency features
Medical and emergency-contact information is stored to help you share critical details with responders during an emergency. You control what you enter and can edit or delete it at any time.

## 4. Location
Location is used for core functionality. You can revoke location permission in your device settings; some features (navigation, nearby search) will not work without it.

## 5. Data retention & deletion
- Local data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
- Account/cloud data can be deleted from account settings; deletion removes synchronized records from our cloud provider.

## 6. Sharing
We share data only with infrastructure providers necessary to operate the App (e.g. Supabase for cloud sync; map, routing, and weather data providers for requests you initiate). These process data to deliver the requested functionality.

## 7. Security
- Auth tokens are stored using platform secure storage (Keychain / encrypted storage).
- Network traffic uses TLS; high-value endpoints support certificate pinning.
- Telemetry is sanitized before transmission and is opt-in.

## 8. Children
Rihla is not directed to children under the age required by local law for data processing.

## 9. Your rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Use in-app controls or contact us.

## 10. Changes
We will update this policy as needed and revise the "Last updated" date.

## 11. Contact
Provide a support email/URL before submission.
