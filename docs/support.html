Support & contact

We aim to reply within 2 business days.

Get help

Email support@rihla.app for any question, bug, or feedback. You can also use Settings → Send beta feedback inside the app to attach a sanitized diagnostic bundle (with your consent).

Report a bug

Please include:

  • What you were doing and what went wrong
  • Your device model and OS version
  • App version (Settings → About)
  • A screenshot, if possible

Contacts

  • General support: support@rihla.app
  • Privacy requests: privacy@rihla.app
  • Security reports: security@rihla.app
In a real emergency do not wait for app support — contact local services directly. UAE: 999 police · 998 ambulance · 997 civil defence.
