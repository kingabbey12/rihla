Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 June 2026 · Effective on first public release

This Privacy Policy explains how Rihla ("the App", "we") handles your information. Rihla is privacy-first: by default the App does not send analytics or crash data anywhere.

1. Information we process

On your device (not transmitted by default):

  • Location — to show your position, calculate routes, and provide navigation and safety advisories. Used only while you use the App.
  • Medical profile — optional details (blood type, allergies, conditions, medications) to assist emergency responders.
  • Emergency contacts — names and numbers you choose to add.
  • Preferences — theme, language, units, and feature settings.

Cloud sync (only if you create an account): your saved places, preferences, and selected data sync to our cloud provider (Supabase). Authentication tokens are stored encrypted on your device.

Optional analytics & crash reporting (off by default): if enabled in a build, the App may send sanitized product events and crash diagnostics. Personal identifiers, tokens, emails, phone numbers, and precise coordinates are removed before anything leaves the device.

2. How we use information

To provide navigation, search, offline maps, exploration, safety advisories, and emergency features; to synchronize your account data; and to diagnose crashes (only with reporting enabled). We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

3. Emergency features

Medical and emergency-contact information is stored to help you share critical details with responders. You control what you enter and can edit or delete it anytime.

4. Location

Location powers core functionality. You can revoke permission in device settings; some features will not work without it.

5. Data retention & deletion

Local data stays on your device until you delete it or uninstall. Account/cloud data can be deleted from account settings, which removes synchronized records from our cloud provider.

6. Sharing

We share data only with infrastructure providers necessary to operate the App (Supabase for cloud sync; map, routing, and weather providers for requests you initiate).

7. Security

Auth tokens use platform secure storage (Keychain / encrypted storage). Network traffic uses TLS with certificate pinning on high-value endpoints. Telemetry is sanitized and opt-in.

8. Children

Rihla is not directed to children under the age required by local law for data processing.

9. Your rights

You may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Use in-app controls or contact us.

10. Changes

We will update this policy as needed and revise the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

Privacy: privacy@rihla.app · Support: support@rihla.app
