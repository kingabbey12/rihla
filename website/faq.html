Frequently asked questions

Can't find an answer? Email support@rihla.app.

Does Rihla work offline?

Yes. Download an emirate's map area and navigation continues without a connection — useful in tunnels, underground parking, and remote roads.

Is my location and personal data private?

Yes. Analytics and crash reporting are off by default. Your medical profile and emergency contacts are encrypted on your device. Telemetry, when enabled, is sanitized before sending.

What are the Salik and speed-camera alerts?

They are informational advisories to help you plan. They are not guaranteed to be complete or current and never encourage unsafe or illegal driving. Always follow real-world signage.

How does the emergency SOS work?

One tap shares your situation and lets you surface your medical profile and contacts. It is a convenience aid, not a replacement for official services. In the UAE call 999 (police), 998 (ambulance), or 997 (civil defence).

Is the AI copilot accurate?

The AI gives advisory, context-aware answers and can be wrong. Don't rely on it for safety-critical, legal, medical, or financial decisions.

Do I need an account?

No. An account is optional and only used to sync your places and preferences across devices.

Does Rihla support Arabic?

Yes — full Arabic localization and right-to-left layout are supported.

How do I delete my data?

Local data is removed when you uninstall. Account/cloud data can be deleted from account settings, which removes synced records.
