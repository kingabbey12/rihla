# Knowledge Base — Rihla

Public-facing answers (mirrors https://rihla.app/faq) plus internal notes.

## Getting started
- **Install:** Google Play / App Store, search "Rihla".
- **First run:** grant location ("While using"); optionally grant background
  location for uninterrupted navigation, and notifications for alerts.
- **Account is optional** — only needed to sync places/preferences across devices.

## Navigation
- **Offline maps:** download an emirate before traveling; navigation continues
  without signal (tunnels, parking, remote roads).
- **GPS recovery:** after tunnels/underground parking the app re-acquires GPS
  automatically; if stuck, ensure location is set to high accuracy.
- **Background navigation:** keep notifications enabled; battery optimizers on some
  Android devices may kill background location — whitelist Rihla.

## UAE features
- **Salik / speed-camera / driving-rule advisories are informational only.** They
  may be incomplete and never encourage unsafe or illegal driving.
- **Report a map/Salik/camera error:** Settings → Send beta feedback, choose the
  relevant type.

## Emergency
- SOS shares your situation and surfaces your medical profile and contacts.
- **Not a replacement for official services.** UAE: 999 police, 998 ambulance,
  997 civil defence.

## AI copilot
- Advisory answers about your trip; can be wrong. Don't use for safety-critical,
  legal, medical, or financial decisions. Can be disabled via remote config.

## Privacy & data
- Analytics/crash reporting are off by default. Medical profile and contacts are
  encrypted on device.
- **Delete data:** uninstall removes local data; account/cloud data is deletable
  from account settings.

## Troubleshooting
- **Location not working:** check OS permission and that location services are on.
- **Map tiles not loading:** check connection; offline areas need a prior download.
- **Sync issues:** confirm sign-in and connectivity; retry from account settings.
